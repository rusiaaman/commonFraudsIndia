# Import fees due

## Initiation
- You get an sms or an email that import fees is due for your international parcel from some unknown number

## Process
- If you go ahead and click on the link, you're likely to fall for standard phishing or money stealing

## Mechanism
- (Unsure) Your details were sold to fraudsters who then attempt to use the knowledge that you've a parcel due to scam you.
- You may believe it if the logo displayed in email is legit.
- The domain of the link or the email of the sender may look legitimate but it's only similar sounding.
- The origin may be from Zendesk which itself is a trusted company, but anyone can registed on zendesk and use it to send an email.

## Best practices to prevent the fraud
- Know the correct domain name of a company before opening any link.
